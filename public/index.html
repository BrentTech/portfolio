About Me

I come from a successful career in Hospitaliy Management where I gained significant experience in customer interactions, problem solving, and creative thinking during high stress situations. Over the years I realized that I was missing something in my work and it took me quite a while to realize that it was the creative process and producing something. I loved the diversity and opportunities to problem solve every day in Hospitality, and I am passionate about using that experience as a Software Developer. I want to make intuitive products that impact lives, on any level, and make those lives better through technology. Technology should be understandable, approachable, and most importantly usable.
